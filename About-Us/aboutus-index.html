About Us

With the vision in our eyes and the earnest desire to address the issues gripping our society, We, the neighborhood project, search for solutions to these issues.

We hold workshops for slum children, organize collection drives, spread awareness about society's problems, interact with the poor, and provide technical solutions to them. Bit by bit, 'The Neighbourhood Project' has touched upon many lives in the past and will continue to do so in the near future!

We strongly beleive that Every single step with the right motive in the right direction leads to significant differences. We aspire to be the change.
